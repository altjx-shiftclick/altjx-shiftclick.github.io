About ShiftClick

ShiftClick is a real estate investment company specializing in property acquisition, real estate wholesaling, and short-term rental management within designated Opportunity Zones. We work directly with homeowners interested in selling their properties and connect buyers with off-market investment opportunities.

Our Mission

We believe that transparent, straightforward real estate transactions benefit everyone involved. Our mission is to provide homeowners with fair cash offers and flexible closing options, while helping investors find quality properties in growing markets. By focusing on Opportunity Zones, we drive economic growth in areas that benefit most from new investment.

What We Do

ShiftClick provides a range of real estate services, including direct property acquisition with cash offers and quick closings, real estate wholesaling and off-market deal sourcing, short-term rental hosting and management, and property investment within Opportunity Zones. We also offer an SMS alerts program so buyers and sellers can receive personalized text messages about property opportunities, new listings, price updates, open houses, and market trends in their local area.

SMS Alerts Program

Stay informed about real estate opportunities in your area by subscribing to our free SMS alerts. You'll receive personalized text messages about new property listings, cash offer opportunities, open house invitations, and local market updates. You can subscribe at shiftclick.io/register and opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Why Opportunity Zones

Opportunity Zones are federally designated areas designed to encourage long-term investment in underserved communities. By operating within these zones, ShiftClick contributes to local economic development while taking advantage of the growth potential these areas offer.
