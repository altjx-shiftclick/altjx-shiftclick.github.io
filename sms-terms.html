SMS Terms & Conditions

Program Description

ShiftClick uses SMS messaging solely for two-factor authentication (2FA). When you create an account or sign in to a ShiftClick application, you may receive a one-time verification code via text message to confirm your identity.

Opt-In

By providing your phone number and requesting a verification code, you consent to receive SMS messages from ShiftClick for authentication purposes. Consent is not a condition of purchase. You may be asked to confirm your opt-in by replying YES to an initial message.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your authentication activity. Messages are only sent when you initiate a sign-in or account verification request. No marketing or promotional messages will be sent.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will no longer receive SMS verification codes. You may need to use an alternative authentication method to access your account.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any message or contact us at support@shiftclick.io.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Supported Carriers

Supported carriers include but are not limited to AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and other major US carriers. Service may not be available on all carriers.

Privacy

Your phone number and related information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Phone numbers are collected solely for the purpose of account authentication. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
