Program Description

ShiftClick operates an SMS-based messaging program designed for individuals interested in buying, selling, or learning about real estate opportunities in their local market. When you subscribe through our dedicated SMS subscription form at shiftclick.io/register, you will receive text messages related to real estate, including property opportunities, cash offer details, new listing alerts, price updates, open house invitations, market updates, and follow-up communications regarding properties you have expressed interest in.

Message Types

Messages sent by ShiftClick may include both informational and marketing content. The specific message types you may receive include:

  • Property Alerts: Notifications about new property listings, price changes, and available investment opportunities in your area.
  • Cash Offer Outreach: Personalized messages about potential cash offers or quick closing options for properties you own or have expressed interest in selling.
  • Open House Invitations: Invitations to upcoming open houses and property showings.
  • Market Updates: Local real estate market trends, insights, and opportunities.
  • Follow-Up Communications: Personalized follow-ups regarding properties or conversations you have previously engaged with.
  • Service Messages: Opt-in confirmation, responses to HELP or STOP keyword requests, and subscription-related notifications.

Opt-In & Consent

By visiting our dedicated SMS subscription form at shiftclick.io/register, entering your first name and phone number, and actively checking the SMS consent box, you can request enrollment in ShiftClick SMS alerts. This form is used only for SMS subscriptions, does not create an account, and does not combine consent for any other marketing channel. Consent is entirely optional and is not a condition of any purchase or use of service. Below is the opt-in language presented on the subscription page:

The checkbox is not pre-checked. You must explicitly select it to provide consent. If you do not check the consent box, no SMS consent is collected.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You can expect to receive approximately 4 to 8 messages per month, depending on available property opportunities and market activity in your area. Message volume may occasionally be higher or lower based on seasonal trends and local market conditions.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by texting STOP to any message you receive from ShiftClick. You will receive a single confirmation message acknowledging your opt-out request. After opting out, you will no longer receive any SMS messages from ShiftClick. You can re-opt in at any time by visiting shiftclick.io/register and submitting the SMS subscription form again.
